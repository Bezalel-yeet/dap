## Entry Requirements

### Digital & Technology Solutions (all pathways — Software Eng, Data, IT Consultant, Business Analyst)

| Qualification | Requirement |
| ------------- | ----------- |
| A-levels | ABB including Maths or Computer Science (AAB without Maths/CS) |
| Access HE | 60 credits overall; 45 at Level 3 — 30 Distinction + 15 Merit minimum |
| GCSEs | Minimum 5 passes; English **and** Maths at grade C / 4 or above |
| Residency | UK / EEA citizen, or 3+ consecutive years in UK / EEA |

### Applied Finance

| Qualification | Requirement |
| ------------- | ----------- |
| A-levels | AAA — **must include Mathematics** (Pure Maths accepted; General Studies / Critical Thinking not accepted) |
| BTEC | Considered case-by-case; Grade A in A-level Mathematics also required |
| GCSEs | English and Maths at grade C / 4 or above |

### Senior Professional Economist (EMAP — Level 7)

- Must already be employed in an economist role with employer support
- Undergraduate degree in Economics (or closely related discipline)

### Clinical Education PGCert (Level 7)

- 2:2 or above in a medical or health professional degree
- Minimum 1 year of clinical teaching experience
- Currently employed in a relevant NHS / health trust role

## How to Apply

> ⚠️ You apply **to the employer**, not directly to QMUL. QMUL is the academic partner.

1. **Find a vacancy** — [Find an Apprenticeship (GOV.UK)](https://www.gov.uk/apply-apprenticeship) or direct employer sites
2. **Apply to the employer** — written application + personal statement, then assessment centre / interview
3. **QMUL enrolment** — if offered, complete a short QMUL enrolment form to create your student record

**Employer portals for current openings:**

- Goldman Sachs: [goldmansachs.com/careers](https://www.goldmansachs.com/careers/students/programs-and-internships/emea/degree-apprentices)
- Morgan Stanley: [morganstanley.com/people-opportunities](https://www.morganstanley.com/people-opportunities/students)
- Amazon: [amazonapprenticeships.co.uk](https://www.amazonapprenticeships.co.uk)
- Bloomberg: [careers.bloomberg.com](https://careers.bloomberg.com)
- Deutsche Bank: [db.com/careers](https://careers.db.com)
- Reply: [reply.com/careers](https://www.reply.com/en/about/careers)

## Documents to Prepare

- [ ] **CV** — highlight any tech, finance, or relevant work/project experience
- [ ] **Personal statement** — why this apprenticeship, why this employer, career goals
- [ ] **Academic transcripts / predicted grades** — A-level results or predicted grades
- [ ] **GCSE certificates** — English and Maths especially
- [ ] **Right to work evidence** — passport or BRP
- [ ] **References** — at least one academic, one professional if possible
- [ ] **Portfolio / GitHub / project samples** (for DTS pathways — strong differentiator)
- [ ] **Cover letter** — employer-specific, tailored per application
